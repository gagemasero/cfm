Contractor Acknowledgement

This checklist must be completed in full before a startup can be scheduled. CFM Heating will not schedule a startup until this completed checklist has been submitted.

CFM Heating does not accept partial startups.

CFM Heating requests that the startup be coordinated with all parties involved (General Contractor, Electrical Contractor, Controls Contractor, Mechanical Contractor, etc.) so that any issues identified during startup can be addressed and corrected immediately.

Only after successful completion of the startup will CFM Heating schedule Testing and Balancing (T&B).
